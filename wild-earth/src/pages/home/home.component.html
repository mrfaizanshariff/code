WHY US?

Our campsite features a variety of tent accommodations, each equipped with comfortable beds and all the necessary camping equipment. You'll also have access to clean bathrooms and hot showers, a fully-equipped kitchen, and a cozy common area where you can relax and socialize with other campers.

In addition to the stunning natural surroundings, our campsite offers a variety of activities and excursions for you to enjoy. You can go kayaking or fishing in the backwaters, take a nature hike through the nearby forests, or simply relax on the beach and watch the sunset.

Whether you're looking for a peaceful retreat or an adventure-filled vacation, our campsite has something for everyone. We can't wait to have you join us and experience all that the backwaters have to offer.

AMENITIES AT OUR JUNGLE CAMP

At our camping site, we strive to provide all the amenities you need for a comfortable and enjoyable experience. One of the highlights of our campsite is the water front dining area, where you can enjoy delicious meals while taking in the breathtaking views of the backwaters.

For your safety and security, our campsite is fully secured to ensure that you can relax and enjoy your stay with peace of mind. We also offer a barbeque area, where you can cook up a delicious meal and spend quality time with your friends and loved ones.

In your tent, you'll find comfortable beds and blankets to ensure that you get a good night's sleep. We are also a pet-friendly campsite, so you can bring your furry friends along for the adventure.

In the evenings, you can gather around the campfire to roast marshmallows, share stories, and enjoy the cozy atmosphere. For your convenience, we also have clean washrooms and hot showers available on site.

We hope that these amenities will help you feel right at home during your stay at our camping site.
